About

Six Italian Restaurant was founded in Baltimore, Maryland by Group 6, Inc. Our savory Italian comfort food is created using local ingredients and a live wood-grill for that fresh, made from scratch culinary experience. We have an excellent wine list andbelieve in hand crafting specialty cocktails with quality tequila straight from our expansive Tequila Library. We also proudly use traditional cooking techniques, including making our foods and cocktails in small batches to ensure peak flavors when served.
